Logical Operators:

    ! (Logical NOT)
    || (Logical OR) only one of them have positive value, then it'll be true
    && (Logical AND) only BOTH with positive value that'll return TRUE

Bitwise Operators:

    & (bitwise AND)
    | (bitwise OR)
    ^ (bitwise XOR)
    &^ (bit clear, AND NOT)
    << (left shift)
    >> (right shift)

Comparison Operators:

    == (equal)
    != (not equal)
    < (less than)
    <= (less than or equal to)
    > (greater than)
    >= (greater than or equal to)
